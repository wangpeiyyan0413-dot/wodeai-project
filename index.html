Project Guide

This system uses a Pose-Net model to classify your behavior into three emotional states: Calm, Anxious, and Happy.

😐 Calm

Neutral expression, arms at sides, relaxed posture.

Keywords: Still / Natural

😰 Anxious

Face on hands, leaning forward, or repetitive movements.

Keywords: Tense / Inward

😊 Happy

Arms raised, energetic movements, head tilted upward.

Keywords: Open / Energetic

How to play

1. Allow camera access. 2. Stand 1-2 meters away. 3. Hold your pose for 2 seconds.
